Pancakes

Receipee : Baked Pancake

Ingredients

* 1 cup all-purpose flour
* 1 tablespoon baking powder
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 2 tablespoons sugar
* 1 cup milk
* 1 large egg
* 2 tablespoons melted butter
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation

* Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
* In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
* In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
* Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
* Pour the batter into a greased baking dish or oven-safe skillet.
* Add any optional add-ins, if desired.
* Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the pancake is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
* Serve warm with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
